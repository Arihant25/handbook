  • ➡️ Jitsi Random Redirect -- (GitHub repo) ... auto-forwarding to a random jitsi-Server

Jitsi Meet apps​

Next to its native webclient interface, Jitsi Meet is also available as

  • 📲 an open-source Jitsi desktop app, see Jitsi Meet Electron for Desktop (Windows, Mac, and Linux clients available)
  • 📲 mobile apps for Android: Google Play, iOS: Apple App Store, and Android: F-Droid.
